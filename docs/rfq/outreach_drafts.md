## OnRobot application engineering

Subject: 2FG14 custom-finger feasibility - 106+ mm opening and 8 kg parcels

Hello,

We are evaluating the 2FG14 for a teach-once ecommerce packing cell. The standard 105 mm external opening does not satisfy our frozen 106 mm minimum. We prefer 110-120 mm usable opening with procurement margin.

Please propose or validate a manufacturer-supported custom-finger design and confirm opening, minimum closure, finger mass/CoG/inertia, platform moment, force transmission, 8 kg dynamic payload, fragile-item force control, and carton-entry clearance. Our current static moment proxy is 23.1 Nm before custom-finger mass and acceleration.

Please also recommend a supported suction tool for flat sealed cardboard/plastic parcels up to 8 kg and provide CAD, collision, inertial, control-interface, and licensing information for simulation.

Regards,
Tarzan Engineering
